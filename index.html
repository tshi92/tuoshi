I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science, Aalto, supervised by Prof. Mario Di Francesco and Prof. Bo Zhao. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science, CityU, supervised by Prof. Jianping Wang. Before that, I was a research associate at the Tianjin Key Laboratory of Advanced Networking at Tianjin University. I received my PhD degree (in 2021) from the Massive Data Computing Lab, at Harbin Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Jianzhong Li. I also worked as a visiting student at George Washington University from 2019 to 2020, advised by Prof. Xiuzhen Cheng.

My research interests include edge computing, machine learning systems and autonomous driving.
